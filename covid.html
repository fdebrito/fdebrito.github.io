IMPORTANT INFORMATION ABOUT CLASSES DURING COVID

We are back to normal and changing partners during classes as it improves everyone's dance technique by dancing with other people.

However as each individual has a different personal circumstance, not everyone will want to change partners or dance with anyone else other than their partner yet. So we can respect that decision and not get upset if anyone declines a dance.

What we are doing to ensure the class is as safe as possible.

  • Availability of hand sanitiser around the dance floor, for frequent use.
  • Even though Flavio has been triple jabbed, he will carry on doing the Lateral Flow Test before teaching when available.
  • Doors or windows open for ventilation when possible.
  • A Face Cover is optional.

    • Please do not attend classes or Milongas if:

    • You have any possible COVID-19 symptons.
    • You have any cold symptons.
