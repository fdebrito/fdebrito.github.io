Milongas and Events


Monthly Milongas

Monthly Milongas are the perfect venue for all students from the Guildford, Portsmouth and Chichester classes to meet and dance together modern Tango.

It is highly recommended that the beginners also stay in for the Practicas, as it is an opportunity to practice the Dance. I will be assisting anyone during the Practica, so feel free to ask for help if needed.

In the first week of every month, the Guildford, Portsmouth afternoon and Portsmouth evening class formats change to a 1-hour All Levels class, (40 minutes class on Thursday afternoons) followed by a mini milonga. The location, price and start/end times are exactly the same as the regular class.

About

Studied Modern Dance at the University of Curitiba (Brazil) for four years and was an active member of the Palavraçao Drama Company. He joined the Centro de Dança Latina Walmir Secchi school in 1999 as a full time teacher of Argentinean tango and Brazilian dances. At the age of 18, the performance of his first significant choreography won a prize at the Curitiba Dance Festival. Flavio brought his natural and modern dance style and teaching skills to the UK in 2002 and has been teaching in Guildford, Portsmouth and Chichester ever since.

He choreographs 'first dances' for weddings and routines for professional dancers. He is also an established teacher, guest teacher and performer at many events, such as Salsa, Jive and Tango weekends across the U.K. Flavio is an active member of the local community and regularly performs at charity events. He attended private master classes in Buenos Aires with star performers from the renowned Tango Fire Company. He has also attended private dance seminars with Gustavo Naveira & Giselle Anne and Claudio Gonzalez.

Flavio specialises in the production, choreography and performance of dance shows and music videos, West End for a guest performance at the Shaftesbury Theatre and at the Venezuelan Embassy.

He choreographed and performed a show with the world famous Tango Band, Otros Aires, at the Chichester Festival Theatre, and recently been featured dancing his own choreography in Roger Taylor’s (from band Queen) latest music video release “Journey’s End”, and Zingaro Blue band's “Argentina”.
